Techniques for WCAG 2.2

Summary

Techniques are examples of ways to meet Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG). They are not required to meet WCAG. For information, see About WCAG Techniques.

Change Log

A list of new, removed or significantly updated techniques:

  1. 15 July 2020: Removed Flash techniques.
  2. 12 Sept 2019: Added Failure of Success Criterion 2.5.1 due to providing functionality via a path-based gesture without simple pointer alternative
  3. 12 Sept 2019: Added Failure due to inability to deactivate motion actuation
  4. 3 Sept 2019: Added Failure of Success Criterion 1.4.12 due to clipped or overlapped content when text spacing is adjusted
  5. 3 Sept 2019: Added Failure of Success Criterion 4.1.3 due to providing status messages that cannot be programmatically determined through role or properties
  6. 6 Aug 2019: Added Failure of Success Criterion 1.4.10 due to content disappearing and not being available when content has reflowed
  7. 16 Jul 2019: Added Providing single point activation for a control slider
  8. 16 Jul 2019: Added Providing controls to achieve the same result as path based or multipoint gestures
  9. 16 Jul 2019: Added Failure of Success Criterion 1.3.4 due to showing a message asking to reorient device
  10. 16 Jul 2019: Added Using a control to allow access to content in different orientations which is otherwise restricted
  11. 10 Jul 2019: Added Creating a two-color focus indicator to ensure sufficient contrast
  12. 10 Jul 2019: Added Failure of Success Criterion 2.1.4 due to implementing character key shortcuts that cannot be turned off or remapped
  13. 10 Jul 2019: Added Making content on focus or hover hoverable, dismissible, and persistent
  14. 18 Jun 2019: Added Using native controls to ensure functionality is triggered on the up-event
  15. Jun 11 2019: Added Failure of Success Criterion 2.5.6 due to interactions being limited to touch-only on touchscreen devices
  16. 18 Jun 2019: Added Provide conventional controls and an application setting for motion activated input
  17. 25 May 2019: Added Semantically identifying a font icon with role="img"
  18. 26 Apr 2019: Added Failure due to locking the orientation to landscape or portrait view
  19. 26 Feb 2019: Removed F52 from SC 3.2.1 (still attached to SC 3.2.5)
  20. 26 Feb 2019: Added Provide sufficient contrast at the boundaries between adjoining colors
  21. 10 Jan 2019: Added Using the CSS reduce-motion query to prevent motion
  22. 14 Dec 2018: Added Ensuring that a contrast ratio of 3:1 is provided for icons
  23. 30 Nov 2018: Added Using CSS width, max-width and flexbox to fit labels and inputs
  24. 30 Nov 2018: Added F95 of 1.4.13 due to content shown on hover not being hoverable
  25. 13 Nov 2018: Added C34 Using media queries to un-fixing sticky headers / footers
  26. 13 Nov 2018: Added C36 Allowing for text spacing override
  27. 13 Nov 2018: Added C37 Using CSS max-width and height to fit images
  28. 30 Nov 2018: Added G207 Ensuring that drag-and-drop actions can be cancelled
  29. 30 Nov 2018: Added F95 Failure of Success Criterion 1.4.13 due to content shown on hover not being hoverable
  30. 30 Nov 2018: Added F96 Failure of Success Criterion 2.5.3 due to "accessible name" not containing the visible label text
  31. 30 Nov 2018: Added C38 Using CSS width, max-width and flexbox to fit labels and inputs
  32. 14 Dec 2018: Added G207 Ensuring that a contrast ratio of 3:1 is provided for icons
