single pointer

new

A single pointer operation involves the use of a single pointer input, such as a mouse, or a single finger or stylus on a touchscreen. Single pointer operations include taps and clicks, double-taps and double-clicks, long presses, and dragging.

Note: Pointer Events [[!pointerevents]] define a pointer as a hardware agnostic representation of input devices that can target a specific coordinate (or set of coordinates) on a screen, such as a mouse, pen, or touch contact.
