single pointer

new

single pointer input, from a device such as a mouse, or from a single finger or pen on a touchscreen

Single pointer operations include taps and clicks, double-taps and double-clicks, long presses, swiping, dragging, and path-based gestures. In contrast, gestures such as "pinch to zoom" or two-finger swipes are multipoint gestures, as they require two or more pointer inputs - in this case, two fingers on a touchscreen.
