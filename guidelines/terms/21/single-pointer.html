single pointer input, such as a mouse, or a single finger or pen on a touchscreen

Single pointer operations include taps and clicks, double-taps and double-clicks, long presses, swiping, and dragging. In contrast, gestures such as "pinch to zoom" or two-finger swipes are multipoint gestures, as they require two or more pointer inputs - in this case, two fingers on a touchscreen.
